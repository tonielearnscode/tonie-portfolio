Join me on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment! Enhanced Security and Privacy

I doubled down on security with a familiar two-factor authentication and a secret PIN for your journal's eyes only. This tag team of security measures not only keeps your data safe but also wraps you in a cozy security blanket, fostering trust, and keeping you coming back for more app magic!

I kickstarted trust-building with a user-focused survey, pinpointing what users hold dear. Armed with these insights, I wove a user-friendly interface that simplifies log-ins, profiles, and navigation. It's all about crafting an app that feels like a breeze!

Turning Pain Points into Smooth Sailing:

Interviews showed 3 major paint points: Security, privacy, Data backup

SECURITY:
  • Keep the app fresh with regular updates
  • Level up protection with strong passwords and a dash of two-factor authentication for added security!
PRIVACY:
  • Your Data, Your Rules- understand how your data is handled through a clear privacy policy.
  • Take control with privacy settings, where you decide what's collected and what's not.
DATA BACKUP:
  • Manage your progress through social media login
  • Cloud saves for data on the fly!
  • Local backup, because your data deserves a safety net too.
